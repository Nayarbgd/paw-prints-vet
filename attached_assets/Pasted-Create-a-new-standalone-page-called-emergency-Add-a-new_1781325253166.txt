SECTION 3 – WHAT TO DO RIGHT NOW
=========================

Create an emergency action guide.

For each emergency show:

Title
Quick actions
What NOT to do

1. Poisoning
DO:
Remove access to the substance.
Take a photo of the product.
Call immediately.

DON'T:
Do not induce vomiting unless instructed.

2. Difficulty Breathing
DO:
Keep the pet calm.
Minimize movement.
Come immediately.

DON'T:
Do not force food or water.

3. Seizures
DO:
Move dangerous objects away.
Dim lights.
Record the duration.

DON'T:
Do not put your hands in the pet's mouth.

4. Heatstroke
DO:
Move to shade.
Use cool (not ice-cold) water.
Call immediately.

DON'T:
Do not immerse in ice.

5. Hit by a Car
DO:
Move carefully using a towel or blanket.
Keep the pet warm.
Come immediately.

DON'T:
Do not manipulate injured limbs.

6. Bloat / Swollen Abdomen
DO:
Seek immediate treatment.

DON'T:
Do not wait.
